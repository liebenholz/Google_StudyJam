Ahi Tuna Steak with Mango Salsa and Avocado

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 ahi tuna steak (6 oz)
  • 1/2 ripe mango, diced
  • 1/4 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 jalapeno pepper, seeded and minced (optional)
  • 1/4 cup fresh lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 ripe avocado, sliced

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine mango, red onion, cilantro, jalapeno pepper (if using), lime juice, and olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  2. Heat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Season the tuna steak with salt and pepper.
  3. Grill the tuna steak for 2-3 minutes per side, or until cooked to desired doneness.
  4. Serve the tuna steak topped with the mango salsa and avocado slices.

Wine Pairing: Pinot Noir

Calories: Approximately 450 calories

Nutritional Facts:

  • Protein: 40g
  • Fat: 20g
  • Carbohydrates: 25g
  • Fiber: 5 g
  • Sugar: 10g

Chicken Breast with Roasted Vegetables and Lemon-Herb Sauce

Preparation Time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 boneless, skinless chicken breast (4 oz)
  • 1 cup chopped vegetables (such as broccoli, carrots, and zucchini)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon chopped fresh rosemary

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200 °C).
  2. Toss the vegetables with olive oil, salt, and pepper. Spread them on a baking sheet and roast for 15-20 minutes, or until tender.
  3. Meanwhile, season the chicken breast with salt and pepper. Heat a grill or grill pan over medium-high heat. Grill the chicken breast for 5-7 minutes per side, or until cooked through.
  4. In a small saucepan, combine the chicken broth, lemon juice, thyme, and rosemary. Bring to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes, or until slightly thickened.
  5. Serve the chicken breast with the roasted vegetables and lemon-herb sauce.

Wine Pairing: Chardonnay

Calories: Approximately 350 calories

Nutritional Facts:

  • Protein: 30g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 30g
  • Fiber: 5g
  • Sugar: 5 g

Tofu Scramble with Spinach and Tomatoes

Preparation Time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 1 block firm tofu, crumbled
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 cup chopped onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup chopped spinach
  • 1/2 cup chopped tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion and garlic and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  2. Add the tofu , spinach, and tomatoes to the skillet. Cook, stirring occasionally, until the spinach is wilted and the tomatoes are softened, about 5 minutes more.
  3. Stir in the fresh basil and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. Serve the tofu scramble immediately.

Wine Pairing: Sauvignon Blanc

** Calories:** Approximately 250 calories

Nutritional Facts:

  • Protein: 20g
  • Fat: 10g
  • Carbohydrates: 20g
  • Fiber: 5g
  • Sugar: 5g
